    • About Game

    Assassin's Creed is a series of video games. The central premise of the story is based on the rivalry between two ancestral secret societies: the Assassins, who desire peace through free will, and the Templars, who aim to dominate the world and impose order on humanity. Both had an indirect relationship with a species that lived before humans, whose society was destroyed by a gigantic solar storm. Mixing characters and historical fiction with real events and figures, the chronological order of the games begins in 2012, and speaks of Desmond Miles, a young man who, with the help of Animus (a machine that allows to see his "ancestral memories"), explores the memories of some of the most prominent Assassins in history.
