    Ghost of Tsushima is an open-world action adventure game by Sucker Punch Productions, released on July 17, 2020, exclusive to the PlayStation 4 gaming console.[1][2][3] The game is set in feudal Japan, the invasion of Tsushima Island by the Mongol Empire in 1274.[2] The main character of the game is a samurai named Jin Sakai, who defends his homeland by fighting attacking Mongolian knights on the island of Tsushima, where he is from.[4]
